2. Scope and Applicability

2.1 This Privacy Policy governs the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information by Fond in connection with the Service. By creating an account, you acknowledge that you have read and agree to the practices described herein.

2.2 The Service is available exclusively to individuals who are at least 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. The Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) does not apply to the Service because we do not direct the Service to children and we take reasonable steps to prevent minors from creating accounts.

3. Collection of Personal Information

3.1 Account Information

We collect your email address, display name, and password upon registration. Your password is stored as a cryptographic hash; we do not store or have access to your plaintext password.

3.2 Relationship and Profile Data

We collect information you provide during onboarding and profile setup, including: your relationship start date; a shared relationship profile containing details such as how you met and your anniversary; your selected couple dynamic and tone preference for Sage; your content level preference (which governs the nature of AI-generated suggestions); your self-profile containing personal details such as interests and love languages; your responses to the onboarding connection quiz; and your private profile, which contains intimacy-related preferences visible only to Sage and not shared directly with your partner.

3.3 Activity and Interaction Data

We collect data generated through your use of the Service's interactive features, including: daily mood check-ins; connection check-in scores and optional notes; daily question answers; weekly reflections; consent board responses; quest titles, descriptions, and rewards; favor token descriptions; offer proposals; wager terms and outcomes; prediction game answers and guesses; and activity history including categories, titles, and ratings.

3.4 Messages and Media

We collect text messages sent through the in-app chat feature. We collect photos you send in chat or add to the Moments scrapbook; photos are encrypted using AES encryption before being uploaded to storage, with decryption keys managed server-side and accessible only to members of your Couple Account. We collect voice recordings sent in chat; voice recordings are likewise AES-encrypted before upload with server-managed keys. We collect optional captions and location names you attach to Moments photos. Location names are text labels you enter manually; see Section 3.7 for information about GPS data.

3.5 Goals, Milestones, and Time Capsules

We collect couple goals and wishlist items including titles, categories, and notes; relationship milestones including dates, titles, and notes; time capsule messages you compose for future delivery; and shared calendar events including dates, titles, and notes.

3.6 Sage Conversation Data

We store the messages you send to Sage and Sage's replies in order to maintain conversational context across sessions. We periodically generate a synthesized memory summary from your Sage conversation history and store that summary to enable more personalized responses over time. If your Couple Account has chat scanning enabled (see Section 7), we store a weekly summary of relationship signals extracted from your chat messages and use it to inform Sage's context.

3.7 Technical and Device Data

We collect your device push notification token to deliver in-app notifications. We collect standard record-level timestamps generated by the Service, such as creation and update times, which are used for features including streak tracking.

3.8 Location Data

We collect GPS coordinates only when you use the venue search feature to find nearby date ideas. Your coordinates are transmitted to our server solely to query a venue directory and are not stored in our database. We do not track your location over time.

3.9 Data We Do Not Collect

We do not access your device contacts, microphone (except for voice recordings you explicitly initiate), camera roll (except for photos you explicitly select and share), or browsing history. We do not operate advertising SDKs or behavioral analytics trackers. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing purposes.

4. Use of Personal Information

We use your personal information exclusively to operate, maintain, and improve the Service. Specifically:

4.1 We use your account information to authenticate you, connect you with your partner, and maintain your account.

4.2 We use your profile, activity history, mood check-ins, daily question answers, reflections, consent board data, and conversation history to compile the Sage briefing described in Section 6, enabling Sage to provide personalized coaching.

4.3 We use relevant profile and preference data to generate AI-powered features including activity suggestions, daily prompts, and quest reward ideas, by sending that data to our AI provider as described in Section 5.2.

4.4 We use your push notification token to alert you when your partner sends a message, answers a question, or takes other in-app actions.

4.5 We use check-in data and timestamps to calculate your daily streak and other engagement metrics within the Service.

4.6 We share account identifiers with our subscription provider to validate your Subscription status.

4.7 We may analyze aggregated, de-identified usage patterns to understand how features are used and to improve the Service. We do not review your messages or Content for this purpose.

5. Disclosure of Personal Information to Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information. We disclose it only to the service providers listed below, for the specific purposes described, and under data processing agreements that restrict those providers' use of your data.

5.1 Supabase (United States)

Supabase serves as our database and file storage provider. All data described in Section 3, including your profile, messages, photos, voice recordings, and check-ins, is stored on Supabase infrastructure located in the United States. Supabase's privacy policy is available at supabase.com/privacy.

5.2 Anthropic (United States)

Anthropic provides the AI infrastructure that powers Sage. When you interact with Sage, or when AI-powered features generate suggestions for your Couple Account, we transmit data to Anthropic's Claude API. The data transmitted includes the couple briefing described in Section 6.1, your messages to Sage and Sage's replies, and, if chat scanning is enabled, a transcript of up to 200 recent text messages from the preceding seven days per weekly analysis cycle. Anthropic processes this data to generate responses and does not use it to train its models by default. Anthropic's privacy policy is available at anthropic.com/privacy.

5.3 RevenueCat (United States)

RevenueCat manages in-app Subscription status. We share your user identifier and platform information with RevenueCat for this purpose. RevenueCat's privacy policy is available at revenuecat.com/privacy.

5.4 Apple Push Notification Service and Expo (United States)

We deliver push notifications through Apple's Push Notification Service via Expo. Apple receives your device push token and notification payload in connection with this service. Apple's privacy policy is available at apple.com/privacy.

5.5 Yelp Fusion API (United States)

When you use the venue search feature, your GPS coordinates and search parameters are transmitted server-side to Yelp's API to retrieve nearby venue results. Yelp's privacy policy governs their handling of that data and is available at yelp.com/tos/privacy_en_us.

5.6 Other Disclosures

We may disclose your personal information if required by applicable law, court order, or valid legal process. We may also disclose your information in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of substantially all of our assets, provided that we would notify you in advance and the acquirer would be bound by this Privacy Policy or a successor policy that provides equivalent protections.

6. Sage AI: Data Processing and Disclosures

6.1 Couple Briefing

Sage is an AI relationship coach powered by Anthropic's Claude. When you open a Sage session, our server compiles a couple briefing and transmits it to Anthropic's API along with your new message. The couple briefing includes: your relationship profile and couple dynamic; both partners' display names and self-profiles; your private profile (which your partner cannot view directly); recent mood check-ins, connection scores, and notes; relationship milestones and photo moment captions; daily question answers from both partners; consent board choices; recent activity history and ratings; quests, favor tokens, offers, and wagers; weekly reflections; the last 40 Sage conversation messages for your current mode; your synthesized Sage memory summary; and, if chat scanning is enabled, signals extracted from recent chat messages.

6.2 Sage's reply and your message are stored in our database for use as future context.

6.3 Sage is not a licensed therapist, counselor, or mental health professional. Sage's responses are provided for informational, entertainment, and general relationship support purposes only and do not constitute professional mental health advice, diagnosis, or treatment. We are not liable for decisions made in reliance on Sage's output.

7. Chat Message Scanning

7.1 The Service includes a weekly background feature that analyzes your Couple Account's recent text messages using AI to extract brief relationship signals, such as shared interests, emotional tone, and inside references. These signals are stored in your Couple Account's AI memory and used to inform Sage's coaching context.

7.2 This feature requires your affirmative opt-in. You may disable it at any time by navigating to Settings and toggling off Chat Scanning. Disabling the feature takes effect immediately for future scan cycles; no new messages will be scanned after opt-out.

7.3 Only plain text messages are analyzed. AES-encrypted media, including photos and voice recordings, is never scanned. Each analysis cycle processes a transcript of up to 200 messages from the preceding seven days.

8. Data Retention

8.1 We retain your personal information for as long as your account remains active.

8.2 If you delete your account through Settings, all data associated with your account and your Couple Account, including messages, photos, voice recordings, Sage conversation history, check-ins, and all other Content, will be permanently deleted from our systems. Deletion is irreversible. Allow up to 30 days for deletion to propagate through all backup systems.

8.3 If your partner deletes their account, your Couple Account relationship is dissolved. Content that exists at the Couple Account level, including messages, shared calendar events, and Moments, may be deleted as part of that dissolution.

8.4 Push notification tokens are removed upon sign-out or account deletion.

8.5 Encrypted media files stored with Supabase are deleted as part of account deletion.

8.6 We do not retain data beyond what is necessary to operate the Service and we do not archive deleted accounts.

10. Security

10.1 Photos and voice recordings sent through the in-app chat are AES-encrypted on your device before upload to storage. Decryption keys are managed server-side and accessible only to members of your Couple Account. This architecture provides meaningful protection against unauthorized storage access; it is not end-to-end encryption in the strict cryptographic sense because the keys reside on our servers.

10.2 All data in transit between the App and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.

10.3 Database access is protected by Supabase Row Level Security policies that restrict data access to members of the relevant Couple Account.

10.4 Passwords are hashed using industry-standard methods by our authentication provider.

10.5 No security system is infallible. We cannot guarantee absolute security, and we are not liable for unauthorized access that is outside our reasonable control. If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it to support@milesaheadbrands.com.
